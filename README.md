# Wajh (وَجٌه)

An integrated smart system designed to bridge the gap between educational outcomes and labor market needs. Wajh aims to achieve balance by providing early career guidance and predicting future job trends.

## Core Objective
To reduce the gap between education and the labor market through AI-driven career guidance and data analysis, ensuring students are well-prepared for future opportunities.

## Key Features
* **Early Career Guidance:** AI-based professional interest tests for school students.
* **Academic Recommendations:** Suggesting educational tracks and majors based on student performance and market demand.
* **Graduate Empowerment:** Linking university degrees with market-required skills and professional certifications.
* **Education Sector Support:** Providing insights to improve curricula based on market saturation or shortages.
